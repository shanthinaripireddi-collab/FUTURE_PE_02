# 🛒 Task 2: AI-Based Customer Support Chatbot  

## 🏢 Business Name  
E-commerce Website  

## ❗ Problem Statement  
Customers often face delays in getting responses to their queries. Customer support teams cannot handle a large number of requests at the same time. This leads to poor user experience and dissatisfaction.  

## 🤖 AI Solution  
Artificial Intelligence can be used to build a chatbot that instantly responds to customer queries. It can answer frequently asked questions, assist in order tracking, and provide 24/7 support without human intervention.  

## 💬 AI Prompt  
Generate responses for a customer support chatbot for an e-commerce website. Answer queries related to order status, refunds, cancellations, and product information in a polite and helpful manner.  

## 📤 Expected Output  
The AI will provide instant, accurate, and polite responses to customer queries, helping users with order tracking, refunds, cancellations, and product details.  

## 📌 Conclusion  
This project shows how AI can improve customer service by providing fast, reliable, and efficient support in e-commerce businesses.
